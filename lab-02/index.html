Let's Play the Guessing Game!

Here are the rules of the game:

  1. I am thinking of a number
  2. You need to think of my number too
  3. You have 4 guesses to get my number correct
  4. My number is between 1 and 20
  5. After each guess, I will tell you if you are too high or too low
  6. After 4 wrong guesses, you loose :(

Let the Games Begin!!
